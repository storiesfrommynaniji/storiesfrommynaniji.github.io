A B O U T

Hi! My name is Raj! During Summer 2024 I enrolled onto the BRIGHT Network Couch to Coder programme which taught me the skills to create my own website from scratch. I pondered the question of what to dedicate my website to but it became clear that I wanted to dedicate this site to the strongest female figure in my life - my nani ji.

My nani ji, otherwise known as my grandma, has been visiting the UK from India since August 2024. I saw this an the ideal time to collect some of her incredible stories and to share her journey through life with the world. I hope you enjoy reading these stories as much as I enjoyed interview her.
